WELCOME TO

Barangay San Juan

San Juan is a barangay in the city of San Pablo, in the province of Laguna.
Its population as determined by the 2020 Census was 3,356.
This represented 1.18% of the total population of San Pablo.

Oral Polio Vaccine

Our Barangay offers free polio vaccine.This is protection against polio for our children aged 2 to 5 years.

Clean-up Drive

At the request of our Punong Barangay Chairman Ani Belda, councilor Marcial Emralino Belen conducted a cleanup in his ward. Councilor Armelito Monsalve Ciolo was also there to clean the canals. Also included in said Clean-up are members of Alpha Khappa Rho San Pablo City and Team Orang.

Learn more...

Road Clearing Operations

The representatives of our Barangay San Juan, led by our Chairman Ani Belda, along with the councilors of each ward and the hardworking BPSOs actively joined and helped.

Learn more...

Social Pension Pay-out

Led by our Senior Citizen officers who are; President-Mrs. Liza Belarmino Gutierrez Treasurer-Mrs. Amie Belarmino Secretary-Mrs. Merly Hernandez and others, today the pay out of our 44 social pension beneficiaries took place at the San Pablo City Convention Center.

Learn more...

Interbarkada Basketball Summer League

Under the guidance of our good Chairman Ani Belda together with our Brgy. Member and Councilor Rovic Baron Belarmino- Youth Adviser was led by the Youth Council under the leadership of Sk Dex Alillana formally and officially the opening of the "Interbarkada Basketball Summer League" took place.

Family Planning

Under the leadership of the SPC Students with their President Thalia Bernardo and under the guidance of our Chairman Ani Belda, the orientation regarding the importance of having Family Planning was successfully carried out.

Healthy Lifestyle Living Program

With the cooperation of CWTS SPC teacher Sir Ruel Ajes, Group President Thalia Del Valle Bernardo and SPC students they deliberately went to the office of our Punong Barangay Chairman Ani Belda to conduct a program that will give us more knowledge in having a healthy lifestyle. The Committee on Education Councilor Rovic Baron Belarmino was also there to support the students.
